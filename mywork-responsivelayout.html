Responsive Card Layout

Live Demo

I completed this project in October 2021. It was the first assignment in my Web Design and Interaction class. The goal of the assignment was to design a card and then make a responsive layout using Masonry.

Process

I started by looking at different collections of photos online for some theme inspiration. I ended up going with a technology theme. I then download different sizes and orientations of photos, so I could display a variety of shapes in the layout design. Next, I found a suitable google font “Consolas”. I thought a mono typescript would look good in a technology themed card.

Once I started coding it didn't take too long for things to come together. Since all the cards have the same structure I mainly had to duplicate the cards to as many photos I wanted to display. I created some random headlines for each of the cards, somewhat relating to the image in the card and also added some lorem ipsum text. Last, I made a small robot logo to place on each card.
