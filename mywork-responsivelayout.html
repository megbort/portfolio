Responsive Card Layout

Live Demo

This was an assignment I completed for my Web Design and Interaction class in October, 2021. The requirement of the assignment was to create a responsive card layout using the Masonry jQuery plugin.

Process

I started by looking at different collections of photos online for some inspiration. I ended up deciding to go with a technology theme. I then created a logo, collected all of my assets and chose a google font. I chose photos with different widths and heights to really test the responsiveness of the site.

Once I started coding, it didn't take too long for things to come together. Since all the cards have the same structure I mainly had to duplicate the cards to as many photos I wanted to display. I created some random headlines for each of the cards and added lorem ipsum text. Last I did testing on the responsiveness of the site and made sure everything was working properly.
