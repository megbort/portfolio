About Me

Megan Krenbrink is a Front-End Developer and Designer currently enrolled in the New Media Design and Web Development program at BCIT. In the summer of 2022, she plans on getting an internship and pursuing a career in Web Development.

Megan grew up on the West Coast of Canada in Victoria, British Columbia. In 2013, she decided to move to Vancouver to attend the Financial Management Program at BCIT. She graduated in 2015 and received a Diploma in Technology. She then worked as an Accounting Assistant for 5 years.

The experience from both programs has allowed Megan to develop exceptional skills in problem-solving, organization and design. She also is proficient in coding languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and React.

Megan has a passion for music and has been playing drums for 18 years. In her spare time likes to play and record music with her band Joey Chaos and the Ghosts. Also in her free time, Megan enjoys drawing, reading, and playing video games.

If you have interest in working with Megan, you can reach her on LinkedIn or by email at megan.krenbrink@gmail.com.
